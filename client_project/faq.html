FAQ

Despite there being about 50,000 stray cats in Singapore, less than 140 reside in the Singapore's SPCA as of June 2020. Singatopore recognizes the disorganization surrounding Singapore's stray cat population and strives to provide a centralized source of relief for them.

Singatopore is not endorsed by any of Singapore's governmental organizations; instead, it is an independent, mainly student-led organization whose mission is to deploy community-provided resources, be them voluntarily supplied food, blankets, cleaning supplies, etc..

Singatopore is a collective effort distributed across mainly students, despite assistance from outside the university being welcome. As mentioned on our homepage, the organization is led by Amanda Lim, an NUS student. Some students from the Anglo-Chinese Junior College (ACJC), one of Singapore's most prestigious JCs, contribute additionally.
