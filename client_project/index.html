Singatopore

Singatopore is the leading nonprofit organization dedicated towards supporting Singapore's cats. Singatopore's primary function is to catalog the city's cats and facilitate communication regarding their care and characteristics.

The collective is led by cat advocate and well-regarded commutity volunteer Amanda Lim. Amanda is a senior business analytics student at the National University of Singapore and aspiring software engineer who's mission is to improve the quality of life of the city's stray pets via a community-oriented approach.
