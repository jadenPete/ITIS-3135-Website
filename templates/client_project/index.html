Singatopore

Singatopore is the leading nonprofit organization dedicated towards supporting Singapore's cats. Singatopore's primary function is to catalog the city's cats and facilitate communication regarding their care and characteristics.

The collective is led by cat advocate and well-regarded commutity volunteer Amanda Lim. Amanda is a senior business analytics student at the National University of Singapore and aspiring software engineer who's mission is to improve the quality of life of the city's stray pets via a community-oriented approach.

Meet Amanda

After returning to Singapore from the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill on exchange, conducting her day-to-day around the National University of Singapore brutally reminded Amanda of the dire state of Singapore's stray cat population.

Despite the university being the 11th best in the world for CS and the country consistently ranking as one of the world's most developed countries, Singapore's extremely high stray cat population lacks the attention and care similarly developed nations provide to their pets.

Armed with this realization, Amanda founded Singatopore with her fellow Business Analytics and USP students and declared its goal to be solving this dire issue.
