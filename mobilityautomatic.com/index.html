Mobility

Mobility Automation is a software consulting firm specializing in the development of automated software systems. Through the strategic deployment of a talented and diverse workforce, we optimize for the daunting and unexpected.

By applying acquired knowledge relating to automated testing, code refactoring, and scripting, we free up valuable engineering time for less monotonous tasks, while solving the problem at hand in far less time.

About Jaden Peterson

Personal background

Jaden Peterson, the CEO and founder of Mobility, is a highly motivated, seasoned Software Engineer. While not attending classes, administering his university's Association for Computing Machinery chapter, or serving as a teacher's assistant, Jaden enjoys inline skating, bicycling, spending time with his girlfriend, or an occasional video game.

Professional background

Jaden began programming computers at the age of 10. His self-taught introduction to computer science comprised writing batch script text-adventure games for his hand-me-down desktop computer. He progressed to developing Windows GUI applications, Android apps, Javascript game modifications, OpenGL-powered games, and full-stack web development.

Today, he works as a part-time Software Engineer I at Lucid Software—whose products include Lucidchart, Lucidspark, and Lucidscale, and are used by 99% of the Fortune 500.

Academic background

Currently, Jaden is a second-year, junior student at the University of North Carolina, Charlotte, where he is majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics. Jaden holds a 4.0 GPA this semester and is enrolled in seven courses, of which Introduction to Modern Algebra and Computer Architecture are his favorites. He is also a TA in Software Engineering.
